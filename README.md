Uber Data Analytics Dashboard

Description

Ce projet consiste en la création d’un tableau de bord analytique basé sur des données Uber afin de répondre à des problématiques métier réelles liées à la performance, à la rentabilité et à la prise de décision.

L’objectif principal est d’analyser les réservations, les revenus, l’utilisation des véhicules et l’expérience client afin d’identifier des leviers d’optimisation opérationnelle.

⸻

Business Problems Addressed
	•	Identifier les causes des réservations perdues
	•	Mesurer la performance globale de l’activité Uber
	•	Déterminer les types de véhicules les plus rentables
	•	Analyser l’évolution des réservations et du chiffre d’affaires dans le temps
	•	Comprendre le comportement des clients et les modes de paiement utilisés
	•	Évaluer la satisfaction des chauffeurs et des passagers

⸻

Dashboard Structure

1. Overview Page

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
	•	Completed Bookings
	•	Lost Bookings
	•	Revenue
	•	Total Distance
	•	Average Distance

Additional analysis:
	•	Vehicle type filter
	•	Monthly and quarterly analysis (bookings and revenue)
	•	Revenue by vehicle type
	•	Top pickup and drop-off locations
	•	Average rider ratings
	•	Average driver ratings

⸻

2. Vehicle Page

Detailed performance by vehicle:
	•	Booking count
	•	Revenue
	•	Contribution to total revenue

⸻

3. Revenue Page

Revenue analysis by:
	•	Customer
	•	Vehicle type
	•	Payment method
	•	Monthly and quarterly trends

⸻

Tools Used
	•	Power BI for data modeling and visualization
	•	Data cleaning and transformation techniques
	•	KPI definition and business-oriented analysis

⸻

Key Outcomes
	•	Improved visibility on booking performance and revenue drivers
	•	Identification of high-performing vehicles and locations
	•	Support for data-driven decision making
	•	Dashboard designed for business and operational stakeholders

Uber Data Analytics Dashboard (English Version)

Description

This project focuses on building an interactive data analytics dashboard using Uber data to address real business challenges related to performance monitoring, profitability, and decision making.

The main goal is to analyze bookings, revenue, vehicle usage, and customer experience to generate actionable insights.

⸻

Business Problems Solved
	•	Understanding booking losses
	•	Monitoring overall business performance
	•	Identifying the most profitable vehicle types
	•	Analyzing booking and revenue trends over time
	•	Understanding customer behavior and payment methods
	•	Evaluating driver and rider satisfaction

⸻

Dashboard Structure

Overview Page
	•	KPIs: completed bookings, lost bookings, revenue, distances
	•	Monthly and quarterly performance analysis
	•	Revenue by vehicle type
	•	Top pickup and drop-off locations
	•	Driver and rider ratings

Vehicle Page
	•	Booking volume by vehicle
	•	Revenue contribution
	•	Vehicle performance comparison

Revenue Page
	•	Revenue by customer, vehicle, and payment method
	•	Monthly and quarterly trends

⸻

Tools Used
	•	Power BI
	•	Data cleaning and transformation
	•	KPI-driven analysis

⸻

Outcomes
	•	Clear insights into revenue and booking performance
	•	Identification of optimization opportunities
	•	Support for business and operational decision making
